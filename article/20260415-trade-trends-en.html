2026 Global Trade Trend Analysis

Date: 2026-04-15

Latest data shows that global trade is recovering, with emerging markets driving growth.

According to the latest report from the World Trade Organization (WTO), global trade is expected to grow by 3.5% in 2026, higher than the 2.8% in 2025. This growth is mainly driven by emerging markets and developing countries, especially in Southeast Asia, Africa, and Latin America.

Digital trade and green trade have become two major trends. With the continuous development of digital technology, cross-border e-commerce and digital service trade are showing rapid growth. At the same time, global attention to sustainable development has also promoted the development of green trade, with increasing demand for environmental protection products and services.

The increase in regional trade agreements is also an important trend. More and more countries are promoting trade liberalization, reducing trade barriers, and improving trade efficiency through signing regional trade agreements.

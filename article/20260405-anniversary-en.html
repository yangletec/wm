Company's 15th Anniversary Celebration

Date: 2026-04-05

TradePro celebrates its 15th anniversary, thanking all customers and partners for their support and trust.

Today, we solemnly held the company's 15th anniversary celebration, reviewing the past 15 years of development and looking forward to future development vision. Over the past 15 years, we have grown from a small trading company to an industry-leading trade solutions provider, which is inseparable from the support and trust of all customers and partners.

At the celebration, the company's founder delivered an enthusiastic speech, reviewing the company's development history, thanking all employees for their hard work, thanking customers for their trust and support, and thanking partners for their close cooperation. At the same time, he also shared the company's future development strategy, emphasizing that it will continue to be customer-centric, constantly innovate, and provide customers with better trade solutions.

A series of wonderful activities were also held at the celebration site, including the company's development history exhibition, excellent employee recognition, customer cooperation case sharing, etc. Customer and partner representatives from all over the country also attended the celebration to witness TradePro's growth and achievements over the past 15 years.

15 years is an important milestone and a new starting point. We will continue to uphold the core values of "integrity, professionalism, innovation, and win-win", continuously improve service quality, expand business areas, and provide more professional and efficient trade solutions for global customers, helping customers achieve business growth and jointly create a better future.

Signing Strategic Cooperation Agreement with European Clients

Date: 2026-03-20

We have reached strategic cooperation with well-known European brands to jointly explore global markets.

Recently, we signed a strategic cooperation agreement with a well-known European brand. The two parties will carry out in-depth cooperation in the field of global trade and jointly explore the international market. This cooperation marks an important step in our business expansion and injects new impetus into the development of both parties.

According to the agreement, the two parties will cooperate in the following areas:

  • Product procurement and sales: The two parties will become each other's priority suppliers and sales channels to achieve resource sharing.
  • Market expansion: Jointly develop emerging markets and share market information and customer resources.
  • Technical exchange: Regular technical exchanges and training to improve the business capabilities of both parties.
  • Supply chain optimization: Cooperate to optimize supply chain management, reduce operating costs, and improve efficiency.

European partners said that choosing to cooperate with us is based on our professional capabilities in the trade field and good market reputation. They believe that through close cooperation between the two parties, they can achieve complementary advantages and jointly create greater business value.

Our CEO also stated that this strategic cooperation is an important part of the company's internationalization strategy. Through cooperation with well-known European brands, we will further enhance our influence in the international market and provide customers with better trade solutions.

The two parties plan to launch specific cooperation projects within the next three months and establish a dedicated cooperation team to coordinate and promote various cooperation matters. We look forward to achieving mutual development through this strategic cooperation and contributing to the prosperity of global trade.
